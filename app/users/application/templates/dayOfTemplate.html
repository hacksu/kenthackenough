We're so glad you're here! Please read over this important information:

 

Bracelets & Photo ID

Please wear your bracelet at all times. You will only be permitted to take food/drinks/snacks if you are wearing your bracelet. Also, if you leave the building at any time during the event, be sure to bring your photo ID with you. Security will not allow you back into the event unless you have a valid photo ID.

 

Event WiFi

Event WiFi is provided by Kent State. All attendees should use this network, even if you attend Kent State University.

Getting Around

We know it can be confusing to find your way around an unfamiliar place!

 

Specific locations for every event can be found on our live page, but here's a brief overview of the important stuff:

  • Library 1st Floor: Opening ceremony, workshops
  • Library 2nd Floor: Check-in, help desk, sponsors, mentors, food and drinks
  • Library 4th Floor: Hacking space, mini events
  • Library 8th Floor: Sleeping area

 

There are also signs all over the library pointing you to specific locations and events. Ask a staff member in a purple T-shirt if you can't find something!

 

Live Page & Slack

Event announcements, the official schedule, and a place to enter your sponsor codes (for points!) can be found on our live page.

 

You can ask questions, connect with mentors, and find team members in our event Slack.

 

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask a staff member in a purple T-shirt. There will also be a help desk open during the entire event on the 2nd floor, where registration was.
 

In Case Of Emergency

If you encounter any kind of life-threatening emergency, please call 911 immediately and then find a staff member or an on-site security guard. For any emergencies that don't fall into this category, please report to a staff member or on-site security.

 

Code of Conduct

We adhere to and enforce the MLH Code of Conduct. If you in any way feel like someone has violated this document, please report it immediately to a volunteer.
